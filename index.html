Celebrating 25 glorious years

Chakrawyuha... is more than an event.. it’s an emotion. Chakrawyuha started in the year 1998 and is stepping into its 25th year. The festival has grown immensely over the years. Just like how a formation of chakravyuha traps the soldier into itself, our CHAKRAWYUHA attracts and traps everyone into its grandeur. A silver lining in the dense dark clouds that’s what Chakrawyuha is for us this year! Celebrating the silver jubilee we are reaching a milestone made of hard work and dedication. The entire team of CHAKRAWYUHA awaits the talent of numerous contingents and have them leave their footprints behind, only to become better next year and, respect and maintain our roots all throughout the event, not only this year but every year that goes by.
